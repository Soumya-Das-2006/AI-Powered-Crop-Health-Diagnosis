Image Guidelines
For Best Results:
  • Use natural daylight (avoid harsh shadows)
  • Focus on affected leaves or plant parts
  • Keep camera steady (avoid blur)
  • Minimum 224x224 pixels (512x512 recommended)
  • Ensure clear symptoms are visible
  • Avoid extreme close-ups or distant shots
Avoid:
  • Blurry or out-of-focus images
  • Very dark or overexposed photos
  • Images taken at night without flash
  • Multiple plants in one frame
Accuracy Policy

This system enforces a 98-100% accuracy standard for treatment recommendations.

≥98% Full diagnosis + treatment
95-97% Informational only
<95% Re-capture recommended

Low confidence results will not provide treatment solutions to ensure farmer safety.
